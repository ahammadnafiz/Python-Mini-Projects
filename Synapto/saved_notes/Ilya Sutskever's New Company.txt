Ilya Sutskever's New Company

Ilya Sutskever, a prominent figure in the field of artificial intelligence, announced the launch of his new company, SSI Inc. The company's mission is to build safe superintelligence, which Sutskever believes is within reach. He describes this endeavor as the most important technical problem of our time.
